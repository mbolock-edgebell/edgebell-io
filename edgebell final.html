<div class="point-card"><div class="point-num">05 — STOCH</div><div class="point-name">Stochastics</div><div class="point-desc">Overbought or oversold readings, plus wide divergence between %K and %D, mark high-probability turning points.</div></div>
<div class="point-card"><div class="point-num">06 — CCI</div><div class="point-name">Commodity Channel Index</div><div class="point-desc">Overbought/oversold readings and zero-line crosses reveal buying and selling pressure before price confirms.</div></div>
